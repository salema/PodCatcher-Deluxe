PodCatcher Deluxe
A webOS 3 "enyo" podcast catcher

Get it from the App Catalog: Simple, Deluxe, Video Deluxe
Notice for the users of Video PodCatcher Deluxe: Unfortunately the current version of webOS has some issues with app-embedded video playback. In most cases, it would simply not show, while audio works okay. For this reason the current version of the Video PodCatcher makes use of the external TouchPad video player instead of the embedded HTML5 video option. Consequently, your experience differs to what you see in the video and on the images in the app catalog. I hope to change the video back to embedded as soon as the issues in webOS are fixed.

Features

Integrated podcast finder, feed URL import, multiple feeds, video/audio playback, seeking, dashboard integration, filter new/old, automatic update and downloads, concurrent downloads, support for password protected feeds, playlists, select all feeds, resume playback on start, support for Bluetooth audio devices, offline listening/watching, user support via mail (see below), and counting...
Some features might not be available in your version of the PodCatcher.
