Netcasts you love, from people you trust. All in one neat app.

All shows from the TWiT Network in one powerful, yet simple app.
Based on the tried and tested Podcatcher Deluxe platform, this app makes it very easy to stream, download, and enjoy all your favorite TWiT shows. This Week in Tech, The New Screen Savers, Security Now!, MacBreak Weekly, and all the other shows are readily available from the human curated in-app catalogue and the feed selection feature will make sure that you get the format that is right for you.

Highlights
Both audio and video playback, fullscreen option
Adaptive layout, works great on both phones and tablets
Resume playback
Notification and lock screen integration
Support for Bluetooth audio devices
Custom download folder
Playlists and filters
Optional auto-downloads and auto-deletes
Synchronization via gpodder.net
No ads, no tracking
